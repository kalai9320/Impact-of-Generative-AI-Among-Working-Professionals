# 🚀 Impact of Generative AI Among Working Professionals

## 📌 Overview

This Executive MBA Capstone Project explores how Generative AI tools are transforming workplace productivity, efficiency, and decision-making. The study analyzes the adoption, benefits, and challenges of AI usage among working professionals through survey-based research and interactive data visualization.

## 🎯 Objectives

🔹 Assess Generative AI adoption among professionals
🔹 Measure productivity and time-saving benefits
🔹 Evaluate user satisfaction with AI tools
🔹 Identify challenges such as privacy and reliability concerns
🔹 Provide business recommendations for AI adoption

## 📊 Dataset

👥 113 Working Professionals surveyed
📝 Structured questionnaire-based data collection
📈 Analysis and visualization performed using Tableau

## 🔍 Key Findings

✅ Over 60% of respondents use AI tools daily
✅ 85% reported satisfaction with AI adoption
⏱️ Most users save 30–60 minutes per day using AI
📈 AI improves productivity, work quality, and task completion speed
⚠️ Data privacy and AI reliability remain key concerns
